Cade Ross + Solana

I collaborated with Solana to craft the brand identity for SolanaU, their university initiative. This endeavor involved designing a cohesive visual language that reflects Solana's iconic brand while appealing to an academic audience. The result successfully bridges the gap between blockchain innovation and educational pursuit.

I began with an extensive research phase, diving deep into Solana's existing brand guidelines, the blockchain education landscape, and current trends in edtech branding. I also conducted interviews with key stakeholders from Solana and potential SolanaU participants to understand their vision and expectations.

Drawing from my research, I developed multiple concept directions that explored different ways to merge Solana's technological prowess with academic tradition. I experimented with various color palettes, typography styles, and iconography that could represent both blockchain concepts and educational themes.

Through several rounds of feedback and refinement, I honed in on a design direction that resonated with both Solana's team and our target audience. I focused on creating a flexible system that could adapt to various applications, from digital platforms to physical marketing materials.

Cade Ross + Zircon

I partnered with Zircon, a startup in the Solana ecosystem, to design their new learning platform. Their goal is to make learning the skills needed to build on the Solana blockchain more accessible.

This project included a complete rebrand, a new marketing website, and the ground-up design of their learning platform. I was responsible for all of the design work, including the logo, branding, and UI/UX design of the platform.

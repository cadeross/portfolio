Cade Ross + Zircon

I partnered with Zircon, a startup in the Solana ecosystem, to design their new learning platform. Their goal is to make learning the skills needed to build on the Solana blockchain more accessible.

This project included a complete rebrand, a new marketing website, and the ground-up design of their new learning platform. I was responsible for all of the design work, including the logo, branding, and UI/UX design of the platform. We began with the brand exploration below.

Brand

When thinking about the Zircon brand, I wanted to explore the essence of creating something new, of exploring and learning, of charting new paths. With these precepts in mind, I embarked on designing three unique identities to present to the founder.

The first I nicknamed "Laboratory". The “Laboratory” mark resembles both a Z and the swirling of a beaker. Through Zircon, users enter its laboratory to grow in Solana and its surrounding ecosystem. This brand brings the overall purpose of Zircon into visual form..

The second identity takes on a rounded look that is modern and joyful. The “Progress!” mark is lean and invokes a feeling of progression, movement, and advancement forward. This concept is integral within the Zircon platform. The mark also takes the shape of a Z, reaffirming the overall identifiability of the Zircon brand.

Lastly, the “Wayfinder” mark resembles a compass that symbolizes how Zircon leads its users through the Solana ecosystem and guides them along their path. Zircon is the gateway and north star for those who want to develop in the Solana ecosystem. The “Wayfinder” mark and identity reflects this core truth.
